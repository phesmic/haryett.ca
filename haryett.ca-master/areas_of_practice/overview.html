Dealing with criminal charges can be a life altering event. Our experts can help you deal with this stressful time.

What do to once you have been charged with a Criminal offense?

It is easy to become overwhelmed when dealing with the criminal justice system. Our duty as Lawyers is to ensure that you have the tools necessary to navigate this situation so that the best possible outcome can be achieved for you and your loved ones.

We have compiled a few answers to frequently asked questions as well as some useful resources to help you get started.

Please remember that every case is different and we recommend talking to us directly to get the most accurate information for your individual case.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Am I going to jail?

This is usually the first question that we are asked by our clients. Incarceration is usually a last resort and can be avoided. Please remember that this is charge specific and that talking to one of our lawyers directly can give you the best answer to this question based on your case.
