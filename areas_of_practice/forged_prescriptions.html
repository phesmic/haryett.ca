What Is A Forged Prescription?

Forging a prescription or possessing a forged prescription is a crime in Alberta. Forging a prescription is defined as illegally acquiring pharmaceuticals for either personal use of for profit. Personal use means that you intend to ingest the medication or compounds for the effect, usually with the intent of getting high or intoxicated. Acquiring them for profit means you intend to sell or distribute them which is a more serious crime. There are several ways to forge a prescription such as blatantly writing your own prescription on a blank pad and increasing quantity on an otherwise legitimate prescription. A skilled Edmonton, Alberta Illegal Prescription Defense Lawyer can help you if you have been charged with forging a prescription.
